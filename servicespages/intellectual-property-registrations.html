What is Intellectual Property Registration?

Intellectual Property Registration is the legal process of obtaining exclusive rights over a brand name, logo, creative work, or other intellectual assets. These registrations help owners prevent unauthorized use and establish legal ownership of their intellectual property.

A properly registered intellectual property asset enhances brand value, strengthens legal protection, and supports long-term business growth.

Key Features of Intellectual Property Registration:

  • Legal protection for brands and creative works
  • Exclusive ownership rights over intellectual assets

  • Protection against unauthorized use and infringement

  • Enhanced business credibility and brand value
  • Supports long-term growth and market recognition
