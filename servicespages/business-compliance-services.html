Business Compliance Services




Introduction

Business Compliance Services ensure that your business adheres to all legal, regulatory, and tax requirements applicable under Indian laws. From GST and TDS compliance to ROC filings and financial reporting, maintaining proper compliance is essential for smooth operations and business growth. At The Tax Titans, we provide end-to-end compliance solutions to keep your business legally secure and hassle-free.

What are the Benefits of Business Compliance Services?

  • Ensures full legal and regulatory compliance
  • Avoids penalties, fines, and legal issues
  • Builds credibility and trust with stakeholders
  • Smooth business operations without disruptions
  • Timely filings and documentation
  • Better financial management and reporting

Who Needs Business Compliance Services?

  • Startups and new businesses
  • Proprietorships, Partnerships & LLPs
  • Private Limited Companies
  • SMEs and growing enterprises
  • Professionals and consultants

What are the Key Areas Covered?

  • GST registration and return filing
  • TDS compliance and filings
  • ROC compliance (for companies & LLPs)
  • Income Tax filings
  • Maintenance of books of accounts
  • Annual filings and regulatory reporting

What Documents are Required?

  • PAN & Aadhaar of business/owners
  • Business registration documents (COI, Partnership Deed, etc.)
  • GST registration details (if applicable)
  • Bank statements
  • Financial records and invoices
  • Previous compliance filings

What are the Penalties & Risks of Non-Compliance?

  • Heavy penalties and late fees
  • Legal notices and prosecution
  • Cancellation of registrations (GST, company, etc.)
  • Business disruptions
  • Loss of credibility and financial risks

Due Dates of Key Compliance

  • GST Returns: Monthly/Quarterly
  • TDS Returns: Quarterly
  • Income Tax Returns: 31st July / 31st October
  • ROC Filings: As per company requirements
  • (Due dates may vary as per applicable laws)
