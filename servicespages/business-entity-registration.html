What is Business Entity Registration?

Business Entity Registration is the legal process of establishing a business under a specific organizational structure recognized by law. Different business entities offer varying levels of ownership control, liability protection, compliance requirements, and taxation benefits.

A properly registered business entity helps build credibility, ensures legal compliance, and provides a strong foundation for future growth and expansion.

Key Features of Business Entity Registration:

  • Selection of the most suitable business structure
