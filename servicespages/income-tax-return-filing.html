What is Income Tax Return (ITR) Filing?

An Income Tax Return (ITR) is a form submitted to the Income Tax Department that contains details of your income, deductions, taxes paid, and tax liabilities for a financial year.

Filing an ITR is important for maintaining tax compliance, claiming refunds, applying for loans, and establishing financial credibility.

Key Features of Income Tax Return Filing:

  • Mandatory compliance for eligible taxpayers
  • Suitable for salaried individuals, businesses, and professionals

  • Helps claim tax refunds and deductions

  • Ensures accurate reporting of income and taxes
  • Supports financial documentation for loans and visas
