E-Invoicing & E-Way Bill Services in India


Introduction

E-Invoicing and E-Way Bill are essential components of GST compliance for businesses involved in the movement and supply of goods. E-Invoicing ensures real-time invoice authentication through the GST portal, while E-Way Bills are required for the transportation of goods beyond specified limits. These systems improve transparency, reduce tax evasion, and streamline business operations.

What are the Benefits of E-Invoicing & E-Way Bill?

  • Real-time invoice validation and authentication
  • Reduction in errors and duplication
  • Seamless generation of GST returns data
  • Faster movement of goods with proper documentation
  • Improved compliance and reduced risk of penalties
  • Better tracking and transparency in transactions

What are the Eligibility Criteria for E-Invoicing & E-Way Bill?

  • Businesses exceeding the prescribed turnover threshold for e-invoicing
  • Registered GST taxpayers involved in B2B transactions
  • Businesses transporting goods exceeding specified value limits
  • E-commerce operators and logistics providers
  • Manufacturers, traders, and service providers (as applicable)

What are the Documents Required for E-Invoicing & E-Way Bill?

  • GSTIN and business details
  • Tax invoice details (buyer, seller, value, HSN, etc.)
  • Transporter details (vehicle number, transporter ID)
  • Invoice number and date
  • Place of supply and delivery details
  • Supporting documents related to goods movement

What are the GST Late Fee & Penalty Details?

  • Penalty for non-generation of E-Way Bill: ₹10,000 or tax sought to be evaded (whichever is higher)
  • Penalties for incorrect or invalid E-Invoicing
  • Detention or seizure of goods in transit for non-compliance
  • Interest @18% per annum on tax liabilities
  • Additional penalties for repeated violations

Due Dates of Compliance

  • E-Invoice must be generated at the time of issuing invoice
  • E-Way Bill must be generated before movement of goods
  • Validity of E-Way Bill depends on distance of transportation
  • Timely updates required in case of changes in transit

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is E-Invoicing under GST?

E-Invoicing is a system where invoices are electronically authenticated by the GST portal and assigned a unique Invoice Reference Number (IRN).

2. What is an E-Way Bill?

An E-Way Bill is a document required for the transportation of goods above a specified value under GST.

3. Is E-Invoicing mandatory for all businesses?

No, it is mandatory only for businesses exceeding the prescribed turnover threshold.

4. What happens if E-Way Bill is not generated?

Goods may be detained, and penalties may be imposed.

5. Can E-Invoice and E-Way Bill be integrated?

Yes, both systems are integrated, making compliance easier and reducing duplication.

6. How long is an E-Way Bill valid?

Validity depends on the distance—generally one day for every 200 km of movement.
