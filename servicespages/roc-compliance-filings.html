What are ROC Compliance & Filings?

ROC (Registrar of Companies) Compliance refers to the mandatory filing of forms, returns, and disclosures that companies must submit to the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) within prescribed timelines.

Regular ROC compliance helps companies maintain transparency, legal validity, and adherence to statutory requirements.

Key Features of ROC Compliance & Filings:

  • Mandatory compliance for registered companies
  • Suitable for Private Limited Companies, OPCs, and other corporate entities

  • Ensures adherence to MCA and Companies Act regulations

  • Helps avoid penalties, notices, and legal complications
  • Maintains company credibility and corporate governance standards
