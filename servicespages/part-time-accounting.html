Part-Time Accounting




Introduction

Part-Time Accounting is a flexible accounting service designed for businesses that need professional financial support without hiring a full-time accountant. It helps manage bookkeeping, expense tracking, invoices, tax records, and financial reporting on a scheduled basis.

Benefits of Part-Time Accounting

  • Cost-effective accounting support for small and growing businesses
  • Professional bookkeeping without full-time salary expenses
  • Accurate maintenance of financial records
  • Timely tracking of income, expenses, invoices, and payments
  • Better cash flow and financial decision-making
  • Helps in GST, TDS, and income tax compliance
  • Reduces accounting errors and compliance risks

Who Should Choose Part-Time Accounting?

  • Small businesses and startups
  • Proprietorship firms, partnerships, LLPs, and companies
  • Businesses that do not require a full-time accountant
  • Professionals, consultants, freelancers, and service providers
  • Retailers, traders, and small manufacturers
  • Businesses looking for regular but limited accounting support

Documents Required for Accounting

  • Purchase and sales invoices
  • Bank statements
  • Expense bills and payment receipts
  • GST registration details, if applicable
  • Previous accounting records
  • Loan statements, if any
  • Payroll and salary details, if applicable
  • Business PAN and basic business details

Services Covered Under Part-Time Accounting

  • Bookkeeping and ledger maintenance
  • Sales and purchase entry management
  • Bank reconciliation
  • Expense tracking and reporting
  • GST data preparation support
  • TDS and basic compliance support
  • Accounts payable and receivable tracking
  • Monthly financial reports and summaries

Penalty & Risk Aspects

  • Incorrect accounting may lead to tax notices or penalties
  • Delayed bookkeeping can create GST and income tax filing issues
  • Poor record management may affect loan approvals and audits
  • Mismatch in books and bank records can create compliance risks
  • Regular part-time accounting helps reduce financial and tax-related errors

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. What is Part-Time Accounting?

Part-Time Accounting is a service where an accounting professional manages your books on a scheduled or limited-time basis.

Q2. Is Part-Time Accounting suitable for small businesses?

Yes, it is ideal for small businesses, startups, freelancers, and firms that do not need a full-time accountant.

Q3. How often will accounting work be done?

It can be done weekly, monthly, or as per your business requirements.

Q4. Can part-time accounting help with GST filing?

Yes, it helps maintain accurate GST records and prepare data for GST return filing.

Q5. Is it more affordable than hiring a full-time accountant?

Yes, it reduces salary and employee-related costs while still providing professional accounting support.

Q6. Can reports be prepared monthly?

Yes, monthly reports and financial summaries can be prepared based on your business needs.
