What is Part-Time Accounting?

Part-Time Accounting is a flexible accounting service where businesses receive professional accounting support on a part-time basis according to their operational requirements. This allows businesses to maintain proper financial records and accounting compliance while reducing overhead costs associated with full-time accounting staff.

It is particularly beneficial for small businesses that need regular bookkeeping and accounting support but do not require a dedicated full-time accountant.
