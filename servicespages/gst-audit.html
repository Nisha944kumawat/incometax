What is GST Audit?

GST Audit is a detailed examination of a business's GST records, returns, and financial statements to verify compliance with GST laws and regulations. The objective is to ensure accurate tax reporting, proper ITC claims, and consistency between GST returns and accounting records.

A properly conducted GST audit helps businesses minimize compliance risks, avoid penalties, and maintain transparency in tax reporting.

Key Features of GST Audit:

  • Comprehensive GST compliance verification
  • Input Tax Credit (ITC) reconciliation and validation

  • Matching of GST returns with books of accounts

  • Identification and correction of compliance gaps
  • Support for annual GST audit and reporting requirements
