What is Income Tax Notice & Scrutiny?

An Income Tax Notice is an official communication issued by the Income Tax Department seeking clarification, additional information, or compliance regarding a taxpayer's financial records or tax returns.

Scrutiny proceedings involve a detailed examination of financial documents and tax filings to verify the accuracy of information reported by the taxpayer.
