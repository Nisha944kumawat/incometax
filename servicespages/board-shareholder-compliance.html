Board & Shareholder Compliance


Introduction

Board & Shareholder Compliance involves managing statutory requirements related to board meetings, shareholder meetings, resolutions, and maintenance of company records as per the Companies Act. Proper governance ensures transparency, accountability, and smooth functioning of the company. At The Tax Titans, we provide complete assistance in managing board and shareholder compliances efficiently and accurately.

What are the Benefits of Board & Shareholder Compliance?

  • Ensures compliance with corporate governance norms
  • Avoids penalties and legal complications
  • Maintains proper statutory records
  • Enhances transparency and accountability
  • Builds investor and stakeholder confidence
  • Smooth decision-making and documentation

Who Needs These Services?

  • Private Limited Companies
  • Public Limited Companies
  • LLPs (for applicable compliances)
  • Startups with multiple shareholders
  • Growing businesses with investors

What are the Key Compliance Areas Covered?

  • Board Meetings and documentation
  • Annual General Meeting (AGM) compliance
  • Extraordinary General Meetings (EGM)
  • Drafting of board resolutions
  • Maintenance of statutory registers
  • Minutes of meetings (Board & Shareholders)
  • Shareholding records and updates

What Documents are Required?

  • Company incorporation documents
  • Register of members and directors
  • Previous board/shareholder meeting records
  • Financial statements
  • Shareholding details
  • Board resolutions and notices

What are the Penalties & Risks of Non-Compliance?

  • Penalties on company and directors
  • Legal notices and regulatory actions
  • Invalid decisions due to improper documentation
  • Loss of investor trust
  • Corporate governance issues

Due Dates & Compliance Timelines

  • Minimum 4 Board Meetings in a year (for companies)
  • AGM: Once every year
  • Timely preparation of minutes (within prescribed time)
  • Event-based compliance as per company decisions
  • (Subject to Companies Act provisions)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is board compliance?

It includes conducting meetings and maintaining records as per legal requirements.

Yes, for all companies.

3. What are board resolutions?

Formal decisions taken by directors during meetings.

4. What happens if meetings are not conducted?

Penalties and non-compliance issues may arise.

5. Can these compliances be outsourced?
