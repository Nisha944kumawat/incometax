What is Stock & Inventory Audit?

Stock & Inventory Audit is a systematic examination of a business's inventory records and physical stock. The objective is to verify inventory quantities, validate inventory valuation methods, identify discrepancies, and ensure proper inventory management practices.

A properly conducted inventory audit helps businesses improve operational efficiency, reduce inventory losses, and maintain accurate financial reporting.

Key Features of Stock & Inventory Audit:

  • Comprehensive physical stock verification
  • Inventory valuation review and validation

  • Identification and reporting of stock discrepancies

  • Detection of inventory losses and leakages
  • Improved inventory control and management efficiency
