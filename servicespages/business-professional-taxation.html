What is Business & Professional Taxation?

Business & Professional Taxation involves the assessment, planning, and management of taxes applicable to businesses, freelancers, consultants, and self-employed professionals.

Effective tax management helps maximize profitability, reduce unnecessary tax burdens, and maintain compliance with income tax regulations.
