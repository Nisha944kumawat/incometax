What are TDS & Compliance Services?

TDS & Compliance Services involve the management of tax deductions, filing of TDS returns, correction of reporting errors, reconciliation of records, and compliance with Income Tax Department regulations.

Proper TDS compliance helps businesses avoid notices, penalties, and interest liabilities while maintaining accurate tax records.
