What is GST Return Filing?

A GST Return is a document filed with the GST Department that contains details of sales, purchases, tax collected, input tax credit claimed, and tax liability for a specific tax period.

Regular GST return filing is mandatory for eligible taxpayers and helps maintain proper tax records while ensuring compliance with GST laws.

Key Features of GST Return Filing:

  • Mandatory compliance for GST-registered businesses
  • Suitable for traders, manufacturers, service providers, and professionals

  • Helps maintain accurate GST records and tax compliance

  • Supports smooth claiming of Input Tax Credit (ITC)
  • Reduces the risk of penalties and notices
