GST Compliance & Advisory Services in India


Introduction

GST Compliance & Advisory services ensure that your business adheres to all GST regulations while optimizing tax efficiency. With constantly evolving GST laws, professional guidance helps businesses stay compliant, avoid penalties, and make informed financial decisions. Our advisory services are designed to simplify complex GST provisions and provide strategic tax planning support.

What are the Benefits of GST Compliance & Advisory?

  • Ensures full compliance with GST laws and regulations
  • Minimizes risk of penalties and notices
  • Optimizes Input Tax Credit (ITC) utilization
  • Provides expert guidance on GST structuring and planning
  • Keeps your business updated with latest GST amendments
  • Improves overall financial efficiency and decision-making

What are the Eligibility Criteria for GST Compliance & Advisory?

  • All GST-registered businesses
  • Businesses facing GST notices or scrutiny
  • Companies with complex transactions (interstate/export/import)
  • E-commerce sellers and service providers
  • Startups and growing businesses needing structured tax planning
  • Businesses seeking to optimize GST liability

What are the Documents Required for GST Compliance & Advisory?

  • GST registration certificate (GSTIN)
  • GST return filings (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, etc.)
  • Sales and purchase invoices
  • Input Tax Credit records
  • Financial statements and books of accounts
  • Notices or communication from GST department (if any)

What are the GST Late Fee & Penalty Details?

  • ₹50 per day (₹25 CGST + ₹25 SGST) for delayed return filing
  • ₹20 per day for NIL returns
  • Interest @18% per annum on delayed tax payment
  • Penalties for incorrect filings or non-compliance
  • Risk of GSTIN suspension or cancellation in severe cases

Due Dates of Filing

  • GSTR-1: 11th of the next month
  • GSTR-3B: 20th of the next month
  • QRMP Scheme: Quarterly returns with monthly payments
  • Annual Return (GSTR-9): 31st December of next financial year
  • Other returns as applicable based on business type

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is GST Compliance & Advisory?

It includes professional support to ensure proper GST filing, compliance, and strategic tax planning.

2. Why is GST advisory important?

GST laws are complex and frequently updated. Advisory helps avoid errors, penalties, and ensures optimal tax management.

3. Can GST compliance reduce tax liability?

Yes, proper planning and ITC optimization can legally reduce tax burden.

4. What happens if GST compliance is not maintained?

You may face penalties, interest, notices, or cancellation of GST registration.

5. Who needs GST advisory services?

Any business registered under GST, especially those with complex or high-value transactions.

6. How often should GST compliance be reviewed?

Regularly—monthly or quarterly depending on business operations.
