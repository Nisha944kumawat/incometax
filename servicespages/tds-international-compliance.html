TDS & International Compliance


Introduction

TDS & International Compliance involves managing tax deductions, cross-border transactions, and regulatory requirements under Indian Income Tax laws and international tax frameworks. It includes TDS on domestic and foreign payments, compliance with Double Taxation Avoidance Agreements (DTAA), and reporting of foreign income and assets. At The Tax Titans, we provide expert solutions to ensure accurate compliance and minimize tax risks.

What are the Benefits of TDS & International Compliance Services?

  • Ensures full compliance with domestic and international tax laws
  • Avoids penalties and interest on non-compliance
  • Proper tax deduction on cross-border transactions
  • Utilization of DTAA benefits
  • Smooth handling of foreign remittances
  • Reduced risk of scrutiny and notices

Who Should Opt for These Services?

  • Businesses making foreign payments
  • Importers and exporters
  • NRIs and foreign nationals
  • Companies dealing with international clients
  • Individuals with foreign income/assets

What are the Key Areas Covered?

  • TDS on domestic and foreign payments
  • DTAA applicability and tax relief
  • Form 15CA & 15CB certification
  • Equalisation Levy compliance (if applicable)
  • Foreign income and asset reporting
  • Withholding tax planning

What Documents are Required?

  • PAN of deductor and deductee
  • TAN details
  • Agreements/contracts for foreign transactions
  • Invoice and payment details
  • Bank remittance documents
  • TRC (Tax Residency Certificate) for DTAA
  • Financial statements and supporting documents

What are the Penalties & Compliance Risks?

  • Interest on delayed deduction or payment
  • Penalties for incorrect TDS deduction
  • Disallowance of expenses
  • Penalties for non-reporting of foreign assets
  • Legal consequences in serious cases

Due Dates of Compliance

  • TDS Payment: 7th of next month
  • TDS Returns: Quarterly filing
  • Form 15CA/CB: Before foreign remittance
  • ITR Filing (for reporting foreign income): 31st July / 31st October
  • (Subject to government updates)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is DTAA?

DTAA helps avoid double taxation on the same income in two countries.

2. Is TDS applicable on foreign payments?

Yes, depending on the nature of payment and DTAA provisions.

3. What is Form 15CA/15CB?

Forms required for reporting foreign remittances.

4. What happens if foreign income is not reported?

It may lead to heavy penalties and legal action.

5. Who needs international tax compliance services?

Anyone involved in cross-border transactions or foreign income.
