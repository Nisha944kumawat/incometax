What is Tax Audit?

Tax Audit is an audit conducted under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act. The purpose of the audit is to examine and verify the books of accounts, turnover, profits, and other financial information maintained by businesses and professionals.

A properly conducted tax audit helps ensure accurate tax reporting, minimizes compliance risks, and supports smooth filing of income tax returns.

Key Features of Tax Audit:

  • Audit conducted under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act
  • Verification of turnover, profits, and financial records

  • Ensures compliance with income tax regulations

  • Supports accurate tax reporting and return filing
  • Reduces the risk of tax-related discrepancies and penalties
