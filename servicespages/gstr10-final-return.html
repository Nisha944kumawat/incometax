What is Final Return (GSTR-10)?

GSTR-10, also known as the Final Return, is a return that must be filed by taxpayers whose GST registration has been cancelled or surrendered. It contains details of stock held, tax liabilities, and other information required for closing GST registration.

Filing GSTR-10 is essential for completing the GST cancellation process and ensuring that all compliance obligations are properly fulfilled.

Key Features of GSTR-10 Filing:

  • Mandatory compliance after GST registration cancellation
  • Suitable for businesses that have surrendered or cancelled GST registration

  • Ensures proper closure of GST compliance obligations

  • Helps avoid penalties and future notices
  • Supports smooth business closure procedures
