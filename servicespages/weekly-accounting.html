What is Weekly Accounting?

Weekly Accounting is a structured accounting process where financial transactions are recorded, reviewed, and reconciled every week. This approach helps businesses maintain up-to-date financial records, identify discrepancies quickly, and improve overall financial control.

Regular weekly accounting ensures better visibility into business performance and allows management to make timely financial decisions.

Key Features of Weekly Accounting:

  • Weekly data entry and accounting record updates
  • Regular reconciliation of financial transactions

  • Systematic expense and income tracking

  • Timely identification of accounting discrepancies
  • Better control and monitoring of cash flow
