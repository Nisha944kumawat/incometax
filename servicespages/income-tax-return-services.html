Income Tax & Return Services


Introduction

Income Tax & Return Services involve the accurate preparation and timely filing of Income Tax Returns (ITR) for individuals, businesses, and professionals. It ensures compliance with tax regulations while optimizing tax liability. At The Tax Titans, we provide reliable, efficient, and hassle-free ITR filing services tailored to your financial needs.

What are the Benefits of ITR Filing?

  • Ensures compliance with Income Tax laws
  • Helps in claiming tax refunds
  • Acts as valid proof of income
  • Facilitates loan and visa approvals
  • Enables carry forward of losses
  • Avoids penalties and legal issues
  • Maintains a clean financial record

What are the Eligibility Criteria for ITR Filing?

You must file ITR if:

  • Your income exceeds the basic exemption limit
  • You want to claim a tax refund
  • You have multiple income sources
  • You own foreign assets or income
  • You are a business owner or professional
  • You want to carry forward losses
  • You fall under specified conditions as per the Income Tax Act

What are the Documents Required for ITR Filing?

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Form 16 (for salaried individuals)
  • Bank statements
  • Investment proofs (80C, 80D, etc.)
  • TDS certificates (Form 16A)
  • Capital gain statements (if applicable)
  • Business financial statements (if applicable)
  • Previous year ITR

What are the ITR Late Fee & Penalty Details?

  • ₹1,000: If income up to ₹5 lakh
  • ₹5,000: If income exceeds ₹5 lakh
  • Interest under Sections 234A, 234B, 234C
  • Losses cannot be carried forward if filed late
  • Possible notices for non-compliance

Due Dates of Filing ITR

  • Individuals (Non-audit cases): 31st July
  • Businesses requiring audit: 31st October
  • Belated/Revised Return: 31st December
  • (Dates may change as per government updates)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Is ITR filing mandatory?

Yes, if your income exceeds the exemption limit or under specified conditions.

2. Which ITR form should I choose?

Depends on your income type (ITR-1, ITR-2, ITR-3, ITR-4).

3. Can I file ITR after the due date?

Yes, but with late fees and restrictions.

4. How long does it take to get a refund?

Usually within 7–45 days after verification.

5. Can I revise my ITR?

Yes, before the deadline for revised returns.
