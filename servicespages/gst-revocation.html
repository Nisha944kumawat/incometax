What is GST Revocation?

GST Revocation is the process of restoring a GST registration that has been cancelled by the GST department. Businesses must fulfill prescribed conditions, rectify compliance defaults, and submit the required application for revocation within the applicable time limits.

Successful GST revocation allows businesses to resume normal operations and continue complying with GST regulations.
