Company Incorporation




Introduction

Company Incorporation is the legal process of registering a business entity under the Companies Act in India. It provides your business with a separate legal identity, limited liability protection, and enhanced credibility. At The Tax Titans, we offer end-to-end company incorporation services, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free registration process for startups and growing businesses.

What are the Benefits of Company Incorporation?

  • Separate legal identity for the business
  • Limited liability protection for owners
  • Enhanced credibility and trust
  • Easy access to funding and investment
  • Perpetual succession (business continuity)
  • Tax benefits and structured growth

What are the Eligibility Criteria for Company Incorporation?

  • Minimum 2 directors (for Private Limited Company)
  • At least 1 director must be an Indian resident
  • Minimum 2 shareholders
  • Unique company name approval
  • Registered office address in India
  • Compliance with Companies Act requirements

What are the Documents Required for Company Incorporation?

  • PAN & Aadhaar of directors/shareholders
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Address proof of directors (utility bill, bank statement)
  • Registered office address proof
  • Rent agreement/NOC (if rented property)
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
  • Director Identification Number (DIN)

What are the Fees & Penalty Details?

  • Government fees depend on authorized capital
  • Professional fees for incorporation services
  • Penalties may apply for incorrect or delayed filings
  • Additional compliance costs post-incorporation

What are the Compliance & Filing Due Dates After Incorporation?

  • Commencement of Business (INC-20A): Within 180 days
  • Annual ROC Filings (AOC-4 & MGT-7): Yearly
  • DIR-3 KYC (Directors): 30th September annually
  • Income Tax Return: As per applicable deadlines

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How long does company incorporation take?

Usually 5–10 working days, depending on approvals.

2. Which is better – Pvt Ltd or LLP?

Depends on business goals, funding plans, and compliance preference.

3. Is office address mandatory?

Yes, a registered office address is required.

4. Can a single person start a company?

Yes, through One Person Company (OPC).

5. Do I need a CA for incorporation?

Professional assistance is recommended for smooth registration and compliance.
