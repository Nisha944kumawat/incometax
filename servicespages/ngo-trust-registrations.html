What is NGO & Trust Registration?

NGO & Trust Registration is the legal process of establishing a non-profit organization under the appropriate legal framework. Depending on the organization's objectives and operational requirements, entities may be registered as a Trust, Society, or Section 8 Company.

A properly registered non-profit organization enhances public trust, ensures regulatory compliance, and supports long-term sustainability and growth.

Key Features of NGO & Trust Registration:

  • Legal recognition of charitable and non-profit activities
  • Enhanced credibility and public trust

  • Compliance with applicable laws and regulatory requirements

  • Eligibility for grants, donations, and funding opportunities
  • Supports long-term organizational growth and sustainability
