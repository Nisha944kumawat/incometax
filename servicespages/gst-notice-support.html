GST Notice & Support




Introduction

Managing GST compliance goes beyond return filing—it includes handling notices, maintaining accurate records, and resolving registration issues. Our GST Notice & Support, Accounting & Compliance Support, and GST Revocation services ensure that your business remains compliant, organized, and protected from legal complications. We provide end-to-end support so you can focus on growing your business without stress.

What are the Benefits of These GST Services?

  • Professional handling of GST notices and queries
  • Accurate bookkeeping and GST-compliant accounting
  • Timely filing and compliance management
  • Assistance in restoring cancelled GST registration
  • Reduction in penalties and legal risks
  • Improved financial clarity and tax efficiency

What are the Eligibility Criteria?

  • GST-registered businesses
  • Businesses that have received GST notices
  • Companies requiring GST-compliant accounting support
  • Businesses with cancelled GST registration seeking revocation
  • Startups, SMEs, and growing enterprises needing compliance management

What are the Documents Required?

  • GST registration certificate (GSTIN)
  • GST returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, etc.)
  • Notice received from GST department (if applicable)
  • Sales and purchase invoices
  • Books of accounts and financial records
  • Bank statements
  • Cancellation order (for GST revocation cases)

What are the GST Late Fee & Penalty Details?

  • ₹50 per day (₹25 CGST + ₹25 SGST) for delayed return filing
  • ₹20 per day for NIL returns
  • Interest @18% per annum on unpaid tax
  • Additional penalties for non-compliance or incorrect filings
  • Risk of GSTIN suspension or cancellation

Due Dates of Filing / Compliance

  • GSTR-1: 11th of the next month
  • GSTR-3B: 20th of the next month
  • GST notice response: Usually within 7–30 days
  • Revocation application: Within prescribed time from cancellation
  • Annual Return (GSTR-9): 31st December of next financial year

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is GST Notice & Support?

It includes handling GST notices, drafting replies, and providing expert guidance to resolve issues.

2. What is GST Accounting & Compliance Support?

It involves maintaining GST-compliant books, managing filings, and ensuring proper documentation.

3. What is GST Revocation?

GST revocation is the process of restoring a cancelled GST registration.

4. Can a cancelled GST registration be restored?

Yes, subject to eligibility and timely filing of revocation application.

5. What happens if GST notices are ignored?

It may lead to penalties, legal action, or cancellation of registration.

6. Why is professional GST support important?

It ensures compliance, reduces risk, and helps manage complex GST requirements efficiently.
