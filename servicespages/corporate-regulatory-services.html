Corporate & Regulatory Services


Introduction

Corporate & Regulatory Services involve ensuring that companies, LLPs, and other business entities comply with statutory and regulatory requirements under corporate laws. From company incorporation to ongoing ROC compliance and advisory, these services are essential for maintaining legal standing and smooth business operations. At The Tax Titans, we provide expert guidance and end-to-end support to help your business stay compliant and growth-focused.

What are the Benefits of Corporate & Regulatory Services?

  • Ensures compliance with corporate laws and regulations
  • Avoids penalties, legal notices, and disqualifications
  • Smooth functioning of business operations
  • Enhances credibility with stakeholders and investors
  • Timely filing of statutory returns
  • Expert advisory on regulatory matters

Who Needs These Services?

  • Private Limited Companies
  • LLPs (Limited Liability Partnerships)
  • Startups and growing businesses
  • Directors and promoters
  • Companies planning expansion or restructuring

What are the Key Services Covered?

  • Company and LLP incorporation
  • ROC filings and annual compliance
  • Director KYC (DIR-3 KYC)
  • Filing of AOC-4 and MGT-7
  • Board resolutions and compliance documentation
  • Changes in company structure (director, address, etc.)
  • Strike off and closure of companies

What Documents are Required?

  • PAN & Aadhaar of directors/partners
  • Address proof of directors and registered office
  • Incorporation documents (COI, MOA, AOA)
  • Financial statements
  • Board resolutions and statutory records
  • Previous ROC filings

What are the Penalties & Risks of Non-Compliance?

  • Heavy late fees and penalties
  • Director disqualification
  • Legal action and notices from authorities
  • Company strike-off
  • Loss of credibility and investor trust

Due Dates of Key Compliance

  • AOC-4 (Financial Statements): Within 30 days of AGM
  • MGT-7 (Annual Return): Within 60 days of AGM
  • DIR-3 KYC: 30th September annually
  • (Due dates may vary as per regulatory updates)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is ROC compliance?

It refers to mandatory filings with the Registrar of Companies.

2. Is ROC filing compulsory every year?

Yes, for all registered companies and LLPs.

3. What happens if ROC filings are delayed?

Heavy penalties and possible disqualification of directors.

4. Can I close a company if not in use?

Yes, through proper strike-off procedures.

5. Do startups also need compliance services?

Yes, compliance is mandatory from incorporation.
