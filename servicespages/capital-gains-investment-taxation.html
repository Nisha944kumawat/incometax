Capital Gains & Investment Taxation




Introduction

Capital Gains & Investment Taxation deals with the tax implications arising from the sale or transfer of assets such as property, shares, mutual funds, and other investments. Proper planning is essential to minimize tax liability and maximize returns. At The Tax Titans, we provide expert guidance to help you manage your capital gains efficiently while ensuring full compliance with tax laws.

  • Proper classification of short-term and long-term gains
  • Utilization of exemptions (Section 54, 54F, etc.)
  • Efficient investment restructuring
  • Compliance with tax regulations
  • Maximization of post-tax returns

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is capital gain?

Profit earned from the sale of a capital asset like property, shares, or mutual funds.

2. What is the difference between short-term and long-term gains?

It depends on the holding period of the asset.

3. Can I save tax on capital gains?

Yes, by investing under specified sections like 54, 54F, etc.

4. Is advance tax applicable on capital gains?

Yes, if your total tax liability exceeds the threshold.

5. Can capital losses be adjusted?

Yes, losses can be set off and carried forward as per rules.
