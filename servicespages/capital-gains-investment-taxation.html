What is Capital Gains & Investment Taxation?

Capital Gains & Investment Taxation involves the calculation, reporting, and planning of taxes arising from the sale or transfer of capital assets such as real estate, shares, mutual funds, and other investments.

Proper tax planning helps investors reduce tax liabilities, utilize available exemptions, and achieve better financial outcomes while remaining compliant with tax laws.
