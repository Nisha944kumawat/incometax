Specialized GST Services in India


Introduction

Specialized GST Services are designed to address complex GST requirements that go beyond routine registration and return filing. These services help businesses manage advanced compliance obligations, resolve technical GST issues, optimize tax efficiency, and ensure smooth operations under evolving GST regulations.

What are the Benefits of Specialized GST Services?

  • Expert assistance for complex GST matters
  • Improved compliance and risk management
  • Optimization of Input Tax Credit (ITC) benefits
  • Reduction in GST-related disputes and penalties
  • Professional guidance on industry-specific GST issues
  • Enhanced business efficiency and regulatory compliance

What are the Eligibility Criteria for Specialized GST Services?

  • GST-registered businesses of all sizes
  • Businesses facing complex GST compliance requirements
  • Exporters and importers requiring GST support
  • E-commerce operators and online sellers
  • Manufacturers, traders, and service providers
  • Businesses seeking advanced GST advisory and compliance solutions

What are the Documents Required for Specialized GST Services?

  • GST registration certificate (GSTIN)
  • GST returns and compliance records
  • Sales and purchase invoices
  • Financial statements and books of accounts
  • Input Tax Credit (ITC) records
  • Business agreements and supporting documents
  • Relevant GST notices or departmental communications (if applicable)

What are the GST Late Fee & Penalty Details?

  • ₹50 per day (₹25 CGST + ₹25 SGST) for delayed return filing
  • ₹20 per day for NIL returns
  • Interest @18% per annum on delayed tax payments
  • Penalties for non-compliance, incorrect filings, or GST violations
  • Risk of GST registration suspension or cancellation in severe cases

Due Dates of Filing / Compliance

  • GSTR-1: 11th of the next month
  • GSTR-3B: 20th of the next month
  • Annual Return (GSTR-9): 31st December of next financial year
  • Other compliance timelines as applicable under GST law
