Monthly Accounting




Introduction

Monthly Accounting is a regular accounting service where business transactions, invoices, expenses, bank entries, and financial records are updated every month. It helps businesses maintain accurate books, track monthly performance, prepare financial reports, and stay ready for GST, TDS, income tax, and other compliance requirements.

Benefits of Monthly Accounting

  • Regular maintenance of business accounts
  • Accurate tracking of monthly income and expenses
  • Helps monitor cash flow and business performance
  • Supports GST, TDS, and income tax compliance
  • Reduces year-end accounting workload
  • Provides monthly financial reports for decision-making
  • Helps avoid errors, delays, and compliance issues

Who Should Choose Monthly Accounting?

  • Small and medium businesses
  • Startups, proprietorship firms, partnerships, LLPs, and companies
  • Freelancers, consultants, traders, and service providers
  • Businesses with monthly sales, purchases, and expenses
  • Businesses registered under GST
  • Owners who need regular financial reports and accounting support

Documents Required for Accounting

  • Sales invoices and purchase bills
  • Bank statements
  • Expense bills and payment receipts
  • GST registration details, if applicable
  • Cash transaction details
  • Vendor and customer payment records
  • Payroll and salary details, if applicable
  • Previous accounting records
  • Business PAN and basic business details

Services Covered Under Monthly Accounting

  • Monthly bookkeeping and ledger maintenance
  • Sales and purchase entry management
  • Bank reconciliation
  • Expense tracking and categorization
  • Accounts payable and receivable tracking
  • GST data preparation support
  • TDS and basic compliance support
  • Monthly financial reports and summaries

Penalty & Risk Aspects

  • Delayed accounting may lead to GST and tax filing errors
  • Missing invoices can create mismatch in GST records
  • Incorrect books may result in tax notices or penalties
  • Poor record management may affect audits and loan approvals
  • Monthly accounting helps reduce compliance and financial reporting risks

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. What is Monthly Accounting?

Monthly Accounting means updating and maintaining business accounts every month.

Q2. Who needs Monthly Accounting?

Businesses with regular monthly transactions, GST compliance, expenses, and reporting needs should choose monthly accounting.

Q3. Is Monthly Accounting suitable for small businesses?

Yes, it is suitable for small businesses, startups, freelancers, traders, and service providers.

Q4. Can GST records be prepared monthly?

Yes, monthly accounting helps maintain accurate sales, purchase, and tax records for GST filing.

Q5. Will I get monthly reports?

Yes, monthly financial summaries and reports can be prepared based on your business requirements.

Q6. Is Monthly Accounting better than yearly accounting?

Yes, monthly accounting keeps records updated regularly and reduces year-end workload and compliance risks.
