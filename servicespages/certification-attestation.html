Certification & Attestation




Introduction

Certification & Attestation services involve verifying and certifying financial documents, statements, and records as per statutory and regulatory requirements. These services are essential for businesses, professionals, and individuals who need authenticated documents for legal, financial, or international purposes. At The Tax Titans, we provide accurate, reliable, and timely certification services backed by professional expertise.

  • Required for legal and regulatory compliance
  • Essential for loans, tenders, and financial transactions
  • Facilitates international business and documentation
  • Reduces risk of rejection or disputes
  • Ensures accuracy and professional validation

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

A document certified by a Chartered Accountant verifying financial details.

2. Is certification mandatory for loans or visas?

Yes, many institutions require certified documents.

3. How long does certification take?

Usually 1–3 working days depending on complexity.

4. Can I get customized certificates?

Yes, certificates can be tailored as per requirement.

5. Is attestation valid internationally?

Yes, depending on the type and purpose of certification.
