What is GST Registration?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) Registration is a mandatory tax registration for businesses that meet the prescribed turnover limits or engage in specific business activities. It enables businesses to collect GST from customers, claim input tax credit, and comply with government regulations.

GST registration is beneficial for businesses seeking expansion, government contracts, e-commerce selling, and improved market credibility.

Key Benefits of GST Registration:

  • Legal recognition as a registered business
  • Eligibility to claim Input Tax Credit (ITC)
  • Improved business credibility and trust
  • Seamless interstate business operations
  • Compliance with GST laws and regulations
