What are Startup & Special Registrations?

Startup & Special Registrations are government-issued registrations and licenses that enable businesses to access various benefits, incentives, legal permissions, and recognition programs. These registrations help businesses establish legitimacy while ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

Obtaining the appropriate registrations can improve business credibility, unlock government benefits, and support long-term growth.
